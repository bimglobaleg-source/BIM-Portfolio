ABOUT BIM GLOBAL

One Engineering Intelligence Layer, Built for the Full Asset Lifecycle

BIM GLOBAL is a newly established engineering intelligence practice focused on connecting BIM, GIS, Digital Twin and Big Data into a single coordinated layer — from first design model to day-to-day operations.

HOW WE WORK

Strategic Implementation Partnerships

Rather than handing over a static model and walking away, BIM GLOBAL works alongside owners and operators as an embedded technical partner — aligning modeling standards, data structures, and dashboards to how a facility actually runs.

  • Multidisciplinary BIM coordination from concept through handover
  • GIS and spatial data integrated with the same model environment
  • Digital Twin and Big Data layers built on model-derived data, not siloed systems
  • Facility management and asset lifecycle planning informed by the same source of truth

BIM KNOWLEDGE CORE

BIM Dimensions, 3D Through 7D

Every dimension adds intelligence to the same coordinated model — geometry, schedule, cost, sustainability, and facility management — rather than requiring separate files.

  • 3D — Spatial modeling & coordination
  • 4D — Scheduling & construction sequencing
  • 5D — Cost & quantity extraction
  • 6D — Sustainability & operations
  • 7D — Facility management & asset lifecycle
SITE TO CITY SCALE

GIS Integration, Indoors and Outdoors

Indoor GIS tracks floor networks, utilities, and fire systems inside the building; outdoor GIS covers site boundaries, roads, and terrain. Both feed the same spatial database so a single query can answer questions across the whole asset.

  • Outdoor GIS — site boundaries, roads, terrain
  • Indoor GIS — floor networks, utilities, fire systems
  • Browser-based asset mapping for non-specialist teams
  • Spatial database & analytics shared with the BIM environment
REAL-TIME OPERATIONS

Closing the Loop Between Model and Reality

IoT sensors feed a live data platform that keeps the digital twin model synchronized with the physical asset, surfacing executive-level insight instead of raw sensor noise.

  • IoT sensor ingestion into a single data platform
  • Digital twin model kept in sync with as-built reality
  • Executive dashboards built directly on live model data
